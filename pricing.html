Common questions about our pricing

Do you build websites for businesses in Thika and Kiambu?

Yes — Thika town and Kiambu County are our primary service area, along with surrounding towns like Ruiru, Juja, Gatundu, Githunguri, Limuru and Kikuyu. We also serve Nairobi and businesses across Kenya.

Can I pay via M-Pesa?

Yes. All packages can be paid via M-Pesa. We typically ask for a deposit to start work and the balance on delivery.

What does a revision round include?

Design changes, content updates, layout adjustments, and feature tweaks within the project scope. Each revision round takes up to 5 business days.

Can I upgrade my package after I start?

Yes. You can upgrade from Starter Site to Business Growth, Business Growth to E-commerce Pro, or move to a Custom Solution at any time. We'll credit the amount already paid and continue from where we are.

What happens after the free maintenance period?

You can continue with our Care Plan from KES 2,000/month, or hand off the site to manage yourself. We'll give you a heads-up before anything expires.

Do you provide domain and hosting?

Domain registration and hosting are separate costs — they're not included in the packages above. We'll explain what's available, what it costs, and help you set it up.

Do you have a package for something smaller or custom?

Yes. If none of the packages above fit what you need, message us on WhatsApp and we'll put together a custom quote. No obligation.

Not sure which package is right for you?

Message us on WhatsApp, tell us about your business, and we'll tell you honestly which package fits your budget and goals.
