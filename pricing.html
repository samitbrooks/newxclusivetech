Pricing FAQ

What's included in the revision rounds?

Design changes, content updates, layout modifications, and feature adjustments within the scope of the package. Each revision round is limited to 5 business days.

Can I upgrade my package after purchase?

Yes! You can upgrade from Bronze to Silver or Silver to Gold. We'll credit the difference and add any remaining features and maintenance period.

Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely! If you need something different, contact us to discuss a custom solution tailored to your specific needs and budget.

What happens after the free maintenance period?

We offer affordable maintenance plans starting from KES 2,000/month for 4 hours of support and updates. You'll be notified before expiry.

Do you provide hosting and domain?

Hosting and domain registration are separate. We can help you set up and configure them, or you can use your existing provider. Our packages are for design and development only.

What's the timeline for each package?

Bronze: 2-3 weeks | Silver: 3-4 weeks | Gold: 4-6 weeks. This includes revision rounds. Rush delivery may be available on request.
