About Xclusive Tech

We're a team helping businesses in Thika, Kiambu and the surrounding region get online — with websites that are professional, affordable, and built to bring in customers.

Our story

Founded in 2020, Xclusive Tech started with one straightforward goal: to make professional web design accessible to every Kenyan business, not just the big ones. Too many small businesses in Thika, Kiambu and across Kenya were missing out on customers simply because they didn't have a website — or couldn't afford one.

Since then, we've built over 34 websites for businesses across Kenya — from law firms and logistics companies to coffee brands and healthcare providers. Every one of them is live, serving real customers today.

We publish our prices, tell you exactly what you'll get and when, and you can pay via M-Pesa. That's the deal.

Our mission

To give every Kenyan business — no matter how new or small — access to a professional website at a price that makes business sense. We do that by being transparent about cost, honest about timelines, and focused on results that matter to our clients: more enquiries, more walk-ins, more online sales.

Our vision

A Kenya where every business has a working website — not as a status symbol, but as a practical tool for growth. We're building toward a day when "I can't afford a website" is no longer a reason any Kenyan entrepreneur misses out on customers.

How we work

Four things we commit to on every project

Honest pricing

We publish our prices publicly and don't add surprise costs at the end. Domain and hosting are listed separately — no hidden markups.

Clear timelines

Every package comes with a stated delivery window. We tell you when to expect your site and keep you updated if anything changes.

Mobile-first by default

Over 80% of Kenyan internet users are on a phone. Every site we build works perfectly on a small screen — that's not optional, it's standard.

Local understanding

We know M-Pesa, the local search landscape, and what Kenyan customers expect online. That local knowledge shows up in every site we build.
